# PhonePe Digital Payments Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end Data Analytics solution developed using SQL and Power BI to analyze PhonePe digital payment transactions, customer behavior, revenue trends, and payment performance.

The objective of this project was to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights that help improve customer engagement, transaction efficiency, payment success rate, and overall business performance.

The project combines:
- SQL for data extraction, cleaning, querying, and analysis
- Power BI for dashboard creation and interactive data visualization 

---

# Business Problem
Digital payment platforms process millions of transactions daily. Businesses need effective analytics solutions to:
- Track revenue growth
- Monitor transaction performance
- Understand customer behavior
- Identify high-performing regions
- Improve payment success rate
- Increase customer conversion
- Optimize merchant and payment operations

This project helps solve these business challenges through interactive dashboards and data-driven insights.

# Dashboard Pages

## 1. Executive Overview
This page provides a high-level business summary.

### Key Metrics:
- Total Revenue: 81.30M
- Total Transactions: 7K
- Average Transaction Value: 12.42K
- Total Cashback: 2.08M
- Total Registered Users: 3K

### Key Insights:
- Gujarat generated the highest revenue.
- Revenue fluctuated across different months.
- Bill Payments and Merchant Payments contributed the highest revenue share.

---

## 2. Customers & Users Analytics
This page focuses on customer demographics and user engagement.

### Key Insights:
- Users aged 26–35 generated the highest revenue contribution.
- Female users contributed slightly more revenue compared to male users.
- Average customer rating remained stable at 3.77.
- Realme and OnePlus were among the most used device brands.
- Gujarat and Delhi showed strong app engagement and transaction activity.

### Business Impact:
This analysis helps businesses better understand customer behavior and target high-value customer segments.

---

## 3. Transaction & Payment Analytics
This page analyzes payment methods, transaction status, banking performance, and merchant categories.

### Key Insights:
- UPI was the most preferred payment method.
- Transaction success rate was significantly higher than failed transactions.
- PNB and Kotak contributed the highest banking revenue.
- Fashion and Electronics categories generated the highest merchant revenue.
- Revenue declined in later quarters, highlighting possible seasonal or operational challenges.

### Business Impact:
This analysis helps optimize payment systems and improve transaction efficiency.

---

## 4. Insights & Recommendations
This page converts business findings into strategic recommendations.

### Major Findings:
- Some states had high app opens but low transaction conversion.
- Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities showed strong growth potential.
- The 26–35 age group was the highest spending customer segment.
- Failed transactions highlighted payment optimization opportunities.

### Recommendations:
- Increase merchant onboarding in underpenetrated regions.
- Launch targeted campaigns for high-spending age groups.
- Improve transaction conversion using rewards and cashback programs.
- Collaborate with banks to reduce failed transactions.

---

# Key Business Insights
- Gujarat was the highest revenue-generating state.
- UPI dominated digital payment transactions.
- The 26–35 age group contributed the highest revenue.
- Fashion was the top-performing merchant category.
- Customer engagement was strong, but some regions required better transaction conversion strategies.
